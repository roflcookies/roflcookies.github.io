Global 4001

Super old (60s-80s) parlor guitar made in korea by Global. I got it for free because the bridge was falling off. I used the old 'free steam neck reset' trick to remove some of the belly and straightend the bridge out by pouring boiling water over it and clamping it for several days. It was sold originally with steel strings but I have put on La Bella nylons to keep the tension down. Its a bit quiet but plays nice. The usage of steel strings have worn deep grooves into the fretboard over the decades of service. The neck has various chips and I had to make a tuning button-knob out of sawdust and glue as the G tuner was missing the knob.
