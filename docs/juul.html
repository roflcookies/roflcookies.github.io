THE_JUUL

Best ecig ever made. Developed by Pax Labs and later spun off into its own entity, the Juul revitilized a dying industry with its use of nicotine salts in a simple POD form factor with fun flavors and a slim device.

The once simple and iconic device now takes a back seat to disposable devices that look more like smartphones than cigarettes. This page recognizes that the Juul not only was cool but continues to be.
